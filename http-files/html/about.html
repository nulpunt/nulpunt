About

0. is an online platform for gathering government and policy documents.

0. aims to make documents available for annotation and sharing through social media.

It is founded on the idea that legitimate democracy is a democracy without secrets.

FAQ

What is an Alpha version?

0. is released in during the alpha phase, meaning that new functionalities and features are still being developed. 0. is an open source platform and aims to involve its users in an early stage of the project.

Can I contribute to the development of 0.?

0. is a project in development, and we encourage users to contribute to 0. either by contributing to the code or by sending in documents. 0. is developed as an open source program on GitHub. For information on contributing or submitting ideas, please e-mail renee@nulpunt.nu

Can I upload documents myself?

Not at this moment. We are working on expanding the functionalities of 0., including the possibility for users to upload their own requested government documents. In the meantime you can send us the documents you deem relevant for 0. through e-mail to admin@nulpunt.nu or to:
0. [Nulpunt]

How does 0. deal with the privacy of its users?

0. uses server side encryption, meaning that all data stored in the server is encrypted, including your password. All documents are stored on 0.'s own servers in The Netherlands, and 0. uses a TLS connection making sure that the connection between the browser and webserver is encrypted.

How are the documents in the 0. database acquired?

The documents currently available in 0. are either retrieved from the public domain or acquired by journalists and researchers via FOIA requests. In Dutch: WOB (Wet Openbaarheid van Bestuur).
